## 📝 Step-by-Step Guide to Login

1. Open your web browser and go to
   👉 https://login.maticpcaccount.com/

2. Enter your **registered email address** and **password**.

3. Click on the **“Login”** button.

4. Once logged in, you can:

   * View your devices
   * Check subscription status
   * Run scans
   * Access support

## 🔒 Forgot Your PC Matic Password?

If you can't remember your password:

* Click on the **“Forgot Password?”** link on the login page.
* Enter your registered email ID.
* Follow the instructions sent to your inbox to reset your password.

Make sure to check your spam or junk folder if you don’t see the email.
